Folk Music Program

Would you like a folk music program for your organizations in North Central West Virginia?

We sing folk songs accompanied by 6 and 12 string acoustic guitars. There is no charge for churches, non-profit organizations, nursing homes, etc.

For more information, please contact us by  email.
